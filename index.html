A game where falling down is the way to bounce back up.

 

The longer you fall, the higher you can go.

Bricole is the only cria left that can absorb the height of a fall and reuse its light energy to stretch back up. Hit rock bottom to launch high up into space and leave the dark energy behind.

 

 

After the events, most of life vanished from the world. Throughout the adventures, discover what it is that you are looking for
